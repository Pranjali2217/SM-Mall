Flex Printing

Flex printing is a method of capturing and converting original art images into different colors and is used in various industries. Additionally, the low cost of deployment along with a longer lifespan, driving the flex market and subsequently the flex printing machine market.

One Way Vision

One Way Vision provides a graphic image to the outside and while still allowing visibility to the exterior from the interior. Its micro-perforated material makes it so you can see an image from the exterior. You can see out, but viewers on the outside only see your graphic.

Features :

  • Weather resistant
  • Excellent ink absorbency
  • Low shrinkage and easy usage

Backlit Flex Boards

Backlit Flex Boards are thick translucent materials widely used for Glow Sign Boards, Backlit Displays, Standees, Signages and Hoarding Advertising.

Features :

  • Glossy and matte surface are both available
  • Weather resistant(UV,rain, typhoon and frost)
  • Anti Flame is avaiable

Vinyl - Foam Sheets

Vinyl Foam sheet printing is probably the most common kind of banner and signage printing used today. When you use vinyl, you will send your sign maker a pattern or outline with the colors you would like to use. The vinyl is loaded into a special machine and will be cut into the pattern you have selected.

Features :

  • Soft & Flexible
  • Thermal Insulator
  • Shock / Impact Absorber
  • Flame Retardant

Hordings

Hoardings or Highway Hoardings is a large outdoor advertising structure, typically found in high-traffic areas such as alongside busy roads and Highways. Hoardings present large advertisements to passing pedestrians and drivers. Typically showing witty slogans and distinctive visuals, billboards are highly visible in the top designated market areas.

Types :

  • Normal Flex
  • Star Flex
  • Backlit Flex

ACP Sheets

Printing to Aluminium Composite (ACP) is now often the standard for the high quality direct to media printing for signage and artwork. Producing long lasting prints that are perfect for outdoor use.

Features :

  • ACP is weather resistant.
  • Material of ACP is very light while also being strong and durable.
  • ACP is easy to clean.
  • ACP is easy to process.

Standees

A standee is a large self-standing display promoting a product or an event. Standees are a great point of sales and offline marketing solution which can be used in a variety of ways.

Types :

  • Table Top Stand
  • Double Sided Roll Up Standees
  • Wooden Roll Up Standees.
